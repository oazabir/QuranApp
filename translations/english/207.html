123: You who believe, fight the disbelievers near you and let them find you standing firm: be aware that God is with those who are mindful of Him.

124: When a sura is revealed, some [hypocrites] say, ‘Have any of you been strengthened in faith by it?’ It certainly does strengthen the faith of those who believe and they rejoice,

125: but, as for the perverse at heart, each new sura adds further to their perversity. They die disbelieving.

126: Can they not see that they are afflicted once or twice a year? Yet they neither repent nor take heed.

127: Whenever a sura is revealed, they look at each other and say, ‘Is anyone watching you?’ and then they turn away- God has turned their hearts away because they are people who do not use their reason.

128: A Messenger has come to you from among yourselves. Your suffering distresses him: he is deeply concerned for you and full of kindness and mercy towards the believers.

129: If they turn away, [Prophet], say ,‘God is enough for me: there is no god but Him; I put my trust in Him; He is the Lord of the Mighty Throne.’
